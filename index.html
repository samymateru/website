Who we are

SuperTech was established in January, 2006 and registered as a private company in Tanzania. Founded to fill in the gap in the Electrical and Telecommunications industry locally with the sole intent of providing specialized, quality services to operators in Tanzania, we have seen the gap and spread to offer our services to Africa as a whole. We provide our customer with significant business opportunities and solutions that would benefit them as per their requirements and specifications.
