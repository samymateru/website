WHO WE ARE

Supertech Limited was incorporated as a Limited Company under ordinance (Cap.212) on the 9th January, 2006 with certificate number 55155. The firm is registered by Contractors Registration Board as CLASS FIVE Electrical Contactor with registration number E5/0070/05/2006. It is also registered with the same board as CLASS THREE Specialist contractor (Telecommunications) with registration number SPE3/0273/05/2006. Supertech founded to fill in the gap in the Electrical and Telecommunications industry locally with the sole intent of providing specialized, quality services to operators in Tanzania, we have seen the gap and spread to offer our services to Africa as a whole. We provide our customer with significant business opportunities and solutions that would benefit them as per their requirements and specifications.
