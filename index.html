OPTICAL FIBRE INSTALLATION

Supertech Limited,also specializes in telecommunications/fiber optic installation, splicing, testing, trouble shooting, design and engineering. We have qualified staffs who are ready to response anytime for emergency repairs

Overhead line installation, Our cable placement section is capable in installation of fiber cable (overhead and underground). Supertech Limited has managed to demonstrate her capability by implementing successfully a turn-key project for fiber installation over pole and tower in Tanzania Power Utility (TANESCO) in Dar es Salaam as per detailed projects. We have installed fiber optic cable overhead in the power line for two different scenarios, for short span and free span using wooden pole and high voltage tower respectively.

Fusion Splicing and Testing, Supertech has qualified Engineer and Technician who have the ability to splice, test and troubleshoot in Single Mode, Multi-Mode, and DWDM technology.
